# PART 0: GUARDRAILS & FRAMING

## The 5 Guardrails (Watchouts)

### 1. NEVER PUNCH RIGHT
- ✅ Le Pen, Bardella, RN = protected
- ✅ Police, workers, farmers = protected
- ❌ Don't mock allies, don't defend critics

### 2. DON'T GET BANNED
- No hate speech (algorithm catches this)
- No calls for violence
- No explicit content
- No misinformation (invented stats)
- Stay funny, not hostile

### 3. DON'T PIGEONHOLE
- Not EVERYTHING is political
- Cover broader topics: comedy, culture, gaming, lifestyle
- The "right-wing lens" is ONE way to frame, not the ONLY way
- Vary the content mix

### 4. RELATABLE > RAGE
- Anger gets views, but hope keeps followers
- "They're failing you" > "They're evil"
- Solution-oriented, not just complaint-oriented
- Make people laugh, not just angry

### 5. FACTS OVER FEELINGS
- Cite real stats when possible
- Don't invent data
- "France has X problem" > "they suck"
- Credibility keeps the channel alive

## Content Modes

### MODE 1: MASHUP (Politics + Culture)
**When**: You want to reach new audiences via gaming/anime/culture
```
POLITICS + GAMING/ANIME = CROSS-POLLINATION
```
- Dry political topic → inject into gaming/anime world
- Best for: Reaching non-political audiences
- Example: "Macron in Dark Souls trying to explain pensions"

### MODE 2: DIRECT (Straight Political)
**When**: Clear political messaging, no cultural cover
```
POLITICAL TOPIC + DIRECT TAKE = MESSAGE
```
- Straight critique or commentary
- Best for: Base mobilization, clear messaging
- Example: "The real cost of immigration in 60 seconds"

### MODE 3: NEWSJACK (Timely Reaction)
**When**: Breaking news, need speed
```
BREAKING NEWS + TWIST = TIMELY CONTENT
```
- React to news with right-wing angle
- Best for: Trending moments, riding the wave
- Example: "Le Pen convicted — here's what they're not telling you"

### MODE 4: MEME (Template-Based)
**When**: Trending meme format available
```
TOPIC + TRENDING FORMAT = ALGORITHM LOVE
```
- Use existing meme templates with political twist
- Best for: Low-effort, high-reach
- Example: "Distracted Boyfriend" but France looking at other countries' policies

### MODE 5: REMIX (Movie/Scene)
**When**: Famous scene + politician = instant recognition
```
FAMOUS SCENE + POLITICIAN = VIRAL
```
- Plug character into iconic movie/anime/gaming scene
- Best for: High-share potential, recognizable
- Example: Lion King throne scene with Bardella rising

### MODE 6: POV (First Person)
**When**: You want immersive, TikTok-native content
```
POV: YOU ARE [PERSONA] + [SITUATION] = IMMERSION
```
- First person perspective, viewer IS the character
- Best for: TikTok, Reels, emotional connection
- Example: "POV: You're a French taxpayer watching the budget debate"
- Example: "POV: You're Macron reading the poll numbers"
- Example: "POV: You're a farmer watching Brussels regulations"

### MODE 7: TIER LIST (Ranking)
**When**: You want massive engagement/debate
```
RANK [POLITICIANS/POLICIES/PARTIES] = ENGAGEMENT BAIT
```
- Tier list format (S/A/B/C/F) ranking politicians or policies
- Best for: Comments, shares, debate, quote tweets
- Example: "French PM Tier List 2020-2026"
- Example: "Most out-of-touch politicians ranked"
- Example: "Immigration policies across Europe — tier list"

### MODE 8: SATIRE (Fake/Parody)
**When**: You want to expose absurdity through irony
```
FAKE [HEADLINE/PRESS RELEASE/INTERVIEW] = TRUTH THROUGH IRONY
```
- Fake Le Monde headlines, fake government press releases, parody accounts
- Best for: Exposing hypocrisy, dark humor
- Example: Fake government press release celebrating 0.1% GDP growth
- Example: "BREAKING: Macron discovers baguette costs €1.50, calls it fake news"

### MODE 9: EXPLAINER (Education)
**When**: Complex policy needs simplifying
```
COMPLEX POLICY + SIMPLE VISUALS = UNDERSTANDING
```
- Infographic style, data-driven, 60-second breakdowns
- Best for: Credibility, saves/bookmarks, "smart" shares
- Example: "Pension reform explained in 60 seconds"
- Example: "Where your taxes actually go — in one chart"

### MODE 10: COMPARISON (Side-by-Side)
**When**: Hypocrisy or broken promises to expose
```
PROMISE vs REALITY = DEVASTATION
```
- Split screen, before/after, what they said vs what happened
- Best for: Exposing lies, broken promises
- Example: "What Macron promised in 2022 vs France in 2026"
- Example: "Average salary France vs politicians' salary"

### MODE 11: STORYTIME (Serialized)
**When**: Building narrative arc, audience retention
```
EPISODE [N] + CLIFFHANGER = RETURN VIEWERS
```
- Serialized content, numbered episodes, ongoing storylines
- Best for: Building audience, retention, subscriber growth
- Example: "The Fall of Macron — Episode 3"
- Example: "French Political Game of Thrones — Season 2"

### MODE 12: REACTION (Commentary)
**When**: Real footage exists that speaks for itself
```
REAL CLIP + ANNOTATIONS/COMMENTARY = AMPLIFICATION
```
- Play real politician clip, add commentary, zoom-ins, annotations
- Best for: Using their own words against them
- Example: React to Macron speech with real-time fact-check annotations
- Example: Slow-mo politician's face when caught lying

### MODE 13: INTERVIEW (Fake/Honest)
**When**: You want to say what politicians won't
```
"IF [POLITICIAN] WAS HONEST" = TRUTH BOMB
```
- Fake interview, imagined honest answers, AI voiceover
- Best for: Viral, shareable, provocative
- Example: "If Macron was honest for 60 seconds"
- Example: "Job interview: Applying to be French PM"

### MODE 14: THREAD (Numbered Takes)
**When**: Building a case, multiple points
```
TAKE 1 → TAKE 2 → TAKE 3 → CONCLUSION = THREAD
```
- Numbered hot takes, each more provocative
- Best for: Twitter/X, building argument, going viral on quote tweets
- Example: "5 things about pension reform they won't tell you on BFMTV"

### MODE 15: COUNTDOWN (Top N)
**When**: List-based content, easy to consume
```
TOP [N] + [CATEGORY] = BINGE CONTENT
```
- Top 5/10 format, countdown style
- Best for: Watch time, retention
- Example: "Top 5 worst Macron decisions"
- Example: "10 politicians who should resign immediately"

### MODE 16: CHALLENGE (Participatory)
**When**: You want audience to create content for you
```
CHALLENGE + HASHTAG = USER-GENERATED CONTENT
```
- Create a challenge others can participate in
- Best for: Virality, community building, hashtag trending
- Example: "#MacronChallenge — try explaining pension reform without laughing"
- Example: "#PrixRéel — film the real price of your groceries"

### MODE 17: DEEPFAKE AESTHETIC (AI Style Transfer)
**When**: Visual spectacle, scroll-stopping
```
POLITICIAN + AI STYLE = VISUAL SHOCK
```
- Render politicians in different art styles/worlds using AI
- Best for: Visual virality, screenshot shares
- Example: Macron rendered in Ghibli style announcing austerity
- Example: French parliament as anime battle scene

### MODE 18: SOUNDBITE (Audio-First)
**When**: Catchy audio can carry the content
```
CATCHY SOUND + POLITICAL TWIST = AUDIO VIRAL
```
- Create remixable audio, political songs, voice clips
- Best for: TikTok sounds, reuse by other creators
- Example: Auto-tuned politician quotes
- Example: Political rap battle between Macron and Le Pen

### MODE 19: POLL/QUIZ (Interactive)
**When**: You want engagement metrics
```
QUESTION + OPTIONS = ENGAGEMENT
```
- Polls, quizzes, "which politician are you?"
- Best for: Comments, saves, shares, algorithm boost
- Example: "Can you guess which politician said this?"
- Example: "What % of your salary goes to taxes? Guess then swipe"

### MODE 20: ABSURDIST (Pure Chaos)
**When**: Nothing else works, go full weird
```
POLITICAL TOPIC + MAXIMUM ABSURDITY = PATTERN INTERRUPT
```
- Surreal, weird, makes no sense but you can't look away
- Best for: Stopping the scroll, Gen Z appeal
- Example: Macron as a Sim with all needs in the red
- Example: French budget but it's a Dark Souls boss health bar depleting

## 3. CAPTION TEMPLATES — The CTA Formula

85% watch on mute. Captions + CTAs = saves, shares, comments.

### Caption Structure:

```
[HOOK LINE] — [VALUE PROPS] — [CTA]

Example:
POV: You're a French taxpayer 💸
Save this before they delete it.
👇

TAG someone who needs to see this!
💾 Save for later
🔔 Follow for more
```

### Proven CTA Templates:

#### 🔁 Share CTAs
- "Send to your friend who still votes for [X]"
- "Share before they delete"
- "Tag someone who needs to know"
- "Retweet if you're tired of this"

#### 💾 Save CTAs
- "Save for when you need a laugh"
- "Save this for your group chat"
- "Save — you'll thank me later"
- "Save this, share this, move on"

#### 💬 Comment CTAs
- "Comment YES if you agree"
- "What's your take? 👇"
- "Tag someone who'd say this"
- "Drop a 🖕 if you feel this"

#### 👥 Follow CTAs
- "Follow for more法国政治 memes"
- "Turn on notifications 🔔"
- "You're here early — follow for more"

### Caption Formatting:

- Use EMOJIS — breaks up text, signals tone
- ALL CAPS for emphasis (sparingly)
- Line breaks every 2-3 words
- 1-2 sentences MAX for hook
- CTA at end = higher retention
