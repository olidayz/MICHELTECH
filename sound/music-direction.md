# Music Direction

## Music as Structure
In short-form and ads, music isn't background — it's the skeleton. The beat drop is your Act 3. The build is your rising action. The silence is your beat of realization.

## How to Brief Music

Whether commissioning, licensing, or generating with AI, communicate:

### 1. Mood / Emotion
What should the listener feel? Be specific.
- Not: "happy music"
- Yes: "warm optimism building to triumphant, like the feeling of finishing something you didn't think you could"

### 2. Tempo / Energy
- **BPM range:** Slow (60-80), Medium (80-120), Upbeat (120-140), High energy (140+)
- **Energy arc:** Does it build? Stay flat? Peak and drop? Match to your story/emotional arc.

### 3. Genre / Style
- Lo-fi hip hop (chill, study, casual)
- Orchestral (epic, emotional, cinematic)
- Synthwave (retro, night, drive)
- Acoustic (intimate, raw, folk)
- Electronic/EDM (energy, modern, dance)
- Ambient (atmosphere, meditation, space)
- Jazz (cool, sophisticated, noir)
- Classical piano (elegant, emotional, minimal)
- Trap/hip hop (urban, confident, edge)
- World music (cultural, specific region)

### 4. Instruments
If specific instrumentation matters:
- "Piano only" (intimacy)
- "Strings swell" (emotion)
- "Synth pads" (atmosphere, future)
- "Acoustic guitar" (warmth, folk)
- "Sub-bass" (power, dread)

### 5. Reference Tracks
The fastest way to communicate: "something that feels like [this song] but [different in this way]"

### 6. Timing Sync Points
Where music should align with visuals:
- "Beat drop at 0:15 when the logo reveals"
- "Music softens at 0:08 for the dialogue moment"
- "Build starts at 0:20, peaks at 0:28"

## Music Arc Mapping

Match music to the emotional arc from Storycrafter:

```
Story beat:    Setup    →  Rising   →  Peak    →  Resolution
Music:         Sparse   →  Building →  Full    →  Resolving
Instruments:   Solo     →  Adding   →  All in  →  Stripped back
Energy:        Low      →  Growing  →  Maximum →  Release
```

For shorts/ads, compress this into seconds:
```
0-5s:    Hook — immediate sonic identity
5-15s:   Build — instruments layer in
15-25s:  Peak — full arrangement, maximum energy
25-30s:  Resolve — clean landing, brand moment
```

## Music and Cuts

- **Cut on the beat:** Clean, energetic, rhythmic editing. Standard for music videos and high-energy ads.
- **Cut against the beat:** Slightly off-rhythm cuts create unease, sophistication, or dreamy feel.
- **Let music breathe:** Not every beat needs a cut. Holding a shot through beats creates tension.
- **The drop = the reveal:** If your video has a visual reveal, align it with the musical payoff.

## AI Music Tools

When using AI music generation (Suno, Udio, etc.):
- Describe mood and genre, not just "make a song"
- Specify tempo (BPM) and duration
- Reference instruments
- Generate multiple options and pick the best — AI music benefits from selection
- Edit/trim in post — raw AI output rarely fits perfectly without adjustment
- Check the reference file `ai-tools-current.md` for current tool capabilities
