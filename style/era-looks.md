# Era Looks

Decade-by-decade visual language. Each era has a default look shaped by the cameras, film stock, and aesthetic choices of the time.

---

## 1920s — Silent Film Era
- **Look:** Black and white, iris shots, title cards, exaggerated performance, soft focus, high contrast
- **Cameras:** Hand-cranked, fixed or simple movement
- **Feel:** Theatrical, dreamlike, expressionist, melodramatic
- **Prompt language:** "1920s silent film, black and white, high contrast, soft focus, vignette, iris shot, expressionist, Charlie Chaplin era"

## 1940s — Golden Age Hollywood
- **Look:** Black and white or early Technicolor, studio lighting, glamour, deep focus, three-point lighting
- **Cameras:** Large format studio cameras, controlled dolly moves
- **Feel:** Polished, glamorous, controlled, movie-star allure
- **Prompt language:** "1940s Hollywood, studio glamour lighting, golden age cinema, deep focus, black and white, elegant, classical composition"

## 1950s — Technicolor & CinemaScope
- **Look:** Saturated Technicolor, widescreen, lush color, studio backlots, painted skies
- **Feel:** Epic, romantic, oversaturated beauty, biblical spectacle
- **Prompt language:** "1950s Technicolor, saturated color, widescreen CinemaScope, lush, old Hollywood color, Singin' in the Rain"

## 1960s — New Waves
- **Look:** Breaking rules, handheld, location shooting, mixing B&W and color, experimental editing
- **Feel:** Revolution, youth, freedom, questioning everything
- **Prompt language:** "1960s cinema, handheld, rebellious, location shooting, jump cuts, mod fashion, counter-culture"

## 1970s — New Hollywood / Grit
- **Look:** Desaturated, location shooting, naturalistic lighting, grainy, handheld, real streets
- **Cameras:** Lighter cameras enabling location work, zoom lenses
- **Feel:** Raw, authentic, anti-establishment, urban grit, character-driven
- **Prompt language:** "1970s cinema, desaturated, grainy film, naturalistic, gritty urban, Martin Scorsese, Robert Altman, zoom lens, earth tones"

## 1980s — Neon & Excess
- **Look:** Neon colors, smoke/haze, backlighting, synth-wave palette (purple, pink, cyan), practical lighting
- **Cameras:** Steadicam era, music video influence
- **Feel:** Excess, energy, nightlife, synthwave, MTV, glamour meets grit
- **Prompt language:** "1980s aesthetic, neon pink and cyan, smoke haze, backlighting, synth-wave, MTV era, night, excess, laser, chrome"

## 1990s — Indie & Digital Dawn
- **Look:** Two tracks: indie grit (16mm, grainy, low-budget) and clean blockbuster. Early digital experiments.
- **Cameras:** 16mm indie, first digital (DV cams), plus big studio setups
- **Feel:** DIY authenticity (indie) or glossy spectacle (blockbuster), grunge, minimalism
- **Prompt language:** "1990s indie film, 16mm grain, low budget, natural light, grunge era" OR "1990s blockbuster, clean, Spielberg, digital dawn"

## 2000s — Early Digital
- **Look:** DV cam (Dogme 95, 28 Days Later), early digital cinema, slightly flat compared to film, crisp but less character
- **Cameras:** First digital cinema cameras, consumer DV
- **Feel:** Transitional, sometimes sterile, sometimes revolutionary
- **Prompt language:** "early 2000s digital, DV camera, slightly flat color, digital cinema, early digital film"

## 2010s — Instagram Era
- **Look:** Teal and orange color grading (everywhere), drone shots, hyperlapse, DSLR shallow DOF, anamorphic flares
- **Cameras:** DSLR video revolution, drones, iPhone cinema
- **Feel:** Polished, filtered, social-media influenced, accessible beauty
- **Prompt language:** "2010s cinema, teal and orange grading, shallow depth of field, drone shot, lens flare, Instagram aesthetic, DSLR"

## 2020s — AI-Native
- **Look:** Style-shifting, dreamlike transitions, impossible camera moves, blending real and generated, uncanny beauty
- **Cameras:** AI generation + traditional hybrid, virtual production (The Volume), smartphone cinema
- **Feel:** Post-real, dreamlike, boundary-dissolving, new visual grammar still emerging
- **Prompt language:** "AI-generated cinematic, dreamlike, impossible camera movement, style-shifting, uncanny beauty, hyperreal, 2020s"
