# Texture & Treatment

Post-processing and lens effects that define the feel of the image.

---

## Film Grain
Organic noise from film stock. Different stocks = different character.

| Type | Look | Mood | Prompt |
|------|------|------|--------|
| 8mm grain | Heavy, chunky, uneven | Memory, home movies, decay | "heavy 8mm film grain, organic noise" |
| 16mm grain | Visible, textured, warm | Indie, documentary, authentic | "16mm film grain, textured" |
| 35mm grain | Fine, subtle, cinematic | Classic cinema, professional | "subtle 35mm film grain, cinematic" |
| Digital noise | Sharp, uniform, clinical | Night video, surveillance, low-light | "digital noise, high ISO, low light" |

## Exposure

| Treatment | Look | Mood | Prompt |
|-----------|------|------|--------|
| Overexposed | Blown highlights, bright, washed | Dreamy, heavenly, memory, heat | "overexposed, blown highlights, bright, washed out" |
| Underexposed | Crushed blacks, dark, hidden detail | Mystery, dread, night, poverty | "underexposed, crushed blacks, dark, shadow" |
| Properly exposed | Full range, balanced | Neutral, controlled, professional | (default — don't specify) |
| HDR | Everything visible, flat feel | Documentary, hyperreal, "too clear" | "HDR, high dynamic range, everything visible" |

## Lens Effects

| Effect | Look | Mood | Prompt |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Lens flare | Light streaks, usually from bright source | Epic, romantic, Abrams, Spielberg | "lens flare, anamorphic flare, light streak" |
| Bokeh | Out-of-focus light circles | Romantic, dreamy, intimate, night | "bokeh, shallow depth of field, out of focus lights" |
| Anamorphic | Oval bokeh, horizontal flares, widescreen | Cinematic, epic, movie-feel | "anamorphic lens, oval bokeh, horizontal flare" |
| Chromatic aberration | Color fringing at edges, RGB split | Lo-fi, analog, glitch, vintage | "chromatic aberration, color fringing, RGB edge split" |
| Vignette | Darkened edges, bright center | Vintage, focus, tunnel vision | "vignette, dark edges, center focus" |
| Tilt-shift | Narrow focus band, miniature effect | Miniature world, god's view, playful | "tilt-shift, miniature effect, selective focus" |
| Soft focus | Overall softness, glowing highlights | Romantic, dreamy, beauty, old Hollywood | "soft focus, diffused, glowing, dream" |
| Motion blur | Movement streaks | Speed, time, energy, disorientation | "motion blur, movement streak, long exposure" |
| Prism / kaleidoscope | Fractured, multiplied image | Psychedelic, altered state, surreal | "prism effect, kaleidoscope, fractured image, psychedelic" |

## Color Treatments

| Treatment | Look | Mood | Prompt |
|-----------|------|------|--------|
| Teal & Orange | Hollywood standard, warm skin vs cool background | Commercial, cinematic, safe | "teal and orange color grading, cinematic" |
| Desaturated | Muted, low color | Gritty, realistic, serious, bleak | "desaturated, muted colors, low saturation" |
| Bleach bypass | High contrast, desaturated, metallic | War, grit, intense, Saving Private Ryan | "bleach bypass, high contrast desaturated, metallic" |
| Cross-processed | Shifted colors, unusual tones | Indie, experimental, retro, fashion | "cross-processed color, shifted tones, unusual color" |
| Day for night | Daytime footage graded to look like night, blue tint | Budget night, eerie, slightly off | "day for night, blue-tinted, artificial moonlight" |

| High contrast B&W | Deep blacks, bright whites, no middle gray | Dramatic, stark, graphic, noir | "high contrast black and white, stark, graphic" |

## Double / Multiple Exposure
- **Look:** Two or more images layered on one frame
- **Mood:** Memory, duality, connection between concepts, inner world
- **Prompt language:** "double exposure, overlaid images, merged, ghostly, two worlds combined"

## Light Leaks
- **Look:** Color washes from unintended light hitting film, usually warm orange/red
- **Mood:** Analog warmth, happy accident, summer, nostalgia
- **Prompt language:** "light leak, warm color wash, analog, accidental light, film light leak"

## Long Exposure
- **Look:** Smooth water, light trails, star trails, ghostly movement blur
- **Mood:** Time passing, ethereal, meditative, urban energy (light trails)
- **Prompt language:** "long exposure, light trails, smooth water, star trails, ghostly blur, time passage"
