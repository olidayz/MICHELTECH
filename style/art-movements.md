# Art Movement Aesthetics

Visual styles drawn from art history — powerful for AI generation because models are trained on fine art.

---

## Art Deco (1920s-1930s)
- **Look:** Geometric patterns, symmetry, metallic colors (gold, chrome), sunburst motifs, luxury materials, bold lines
- **Mood:** Glamour, power, modernity, wealth, the future as imagined in the 1920s
- **Prompt language:** "Art Deco, geometric patterns, gold and chrome, sunburst, symmetrical, 1920s glamour, Gatsby, ornate geometric"
- **Reference:** The Great Gatsby (2013), Metropolis, BioShock

## Art Nouveau (1890s-1910s)
- **Look:** Organic curves, floral motifs, flowing lines, stained glass, whiplash curves, feminine, natural forms
- **Mood:** Romantic, organic, decorative beauty, nature worship
- **Prompt language:** "Art Nouveau, flowing organic lines, floral patterns, Mucha style, stained glass, whiplash curves, decorative"

## Surrealism
- **Look:** Impossible juxtapositions, melting objects, dreamscapes, subconscious imagery, reality distorted
- **Mood:** Dreamlike, unsettling, philosophical, subconscious, "more real than real"
- **Prompt language:** "surrealist, Dalí-esque, impossible landscape, melting objects, dreamscape, subconscious imagery, René Magritte"
- **Reference:** Un Chien Andalou, Jodorowsky, David Lynch

## Impressionism
- **Look:** Visible brushstrokes, light emphasis, color over line, atmospheric, soft edges, captured moments
- **Mood:** Fleeting beauty, nostalgia, golden moments, light worship
- **Prompt language:** "impressionist painting, visible brushstrokes, soft light, atmospheric, Monet style, painted, dappled light"

## Ukiyo-e (Japanese Woodblock)
- **Look:** Flat color planes, bold outlines, wave patterns, nature scenes, stylized figures, no perspective
- **Mood:** Elegant, iconic, natural forces, Japanese classical beauty
- **Prompt language:** "ukiyo-e style, Japanese woodblock print, flat color, bold outline, Hokusai, The Great Wave, stylized nature"

## Bauhaus
- **Look:** Primary colors, geometric shapes, functional design, clean typography, form follows function
- **Mood:** Modern, rational, utopian, designed, stripped to essence
- **Prompt language:** "Bauhaus design, primary colors, geometric shapes, clean lines, modernist, functional, Kandinsky, Mondrian"

## Pop Art
- **Look:** Bold colors, Ben-Day dots, commercial imagery, celebrity, mass-produced aesthetic, ironic
- **Mood:** Commercial, ironic, loud, democratic, consumer culture
- **Prompt language:** "pop art, Andy Warhol style, bold flat colors, Ben-Day dots, comic book, Lichtenstein, commercial aesthetic"

## Minimalism
- **Look:** Reduced to essentials, empty space, single colors, geometric, nothing unnecessary
- **Mood:** Calm, focus, intentional, meditative, luxury through absence
- **Prompt language:** "minimalist, negative space, single element, clean, geometric, reduced, essential, white space"

## Brutalism
- **Look:** Raw concrete, massive structures, geometric blocks, unfinished surfaces, monumental
- **Mood:** Power, oppression, institutional, honest materiality, dystopian beauty
- **Prompt language:** "brutalist architecture, raw concrete, massive geometric, monumental, unfinished, institutional, Soviet"

## Gothic
- **Look:** Pointed arches, ornate detail, dark palette, religious imagery, vertical emphasis, stained glass
- **Mood:** Dark romance, spiritual dread, ornate darkness, medieval, haunted
- **Prompt language:** "Gothic, pointed arches, ornate dark, cathedral, stained glass, medieval, dark romance, gargoyles"

## Wabi-Sabi
- **Look:** Imperfect, aged, natural materials, patina, asymmetry, transient beauty, moss and rust
- **Mood:** Finding beauty in imperfection, acceptance, quiet, Japanese philosophy
- **Prompt language:** "wabi-sabi, imperfect beauty, aged texture, natural patina, moss, rust, asymmetric, organic decay, Japanese aesthetic"

## Biomechanical / Giger
- **Look:** Organic forms fused with mechanical, skeletal architecture, alien biology, dark wet surfaces
- **Mood:** Body horror, alien, industrial nightmare, beautiful repulsion
- **Prompt language:** "HR Giger style, biomechanical, organic-mechanical fusion, alien, dark wet surfaces, skeletal architecture, Xenomorph"
- **Reference:** Alien franchise, Giger's art

## Dieselpunk
- **Look:** 1930s-40s technology extrapolated, art deco + war machines, brass and rivets, flying machines
- **Mood:** Alt-history, wartime romance, industrial power, retro-future
- **Prompt language:** "dieselpunk, 1940s retro-future, brass and rivets, propeller aircraft, art deco machinery, wartime, industrial"

## Steampunk
- **Look:** Victorian technology, brass gears, steam, goggles, airships, clockwork, leather
- **Mood:** Adventure, invention, Victorian-future, elegant machines
- **Prompt language:** "steampunk, Victorian, brass gears, steam powered, clockwork, goggles, airship, leather and metal"
