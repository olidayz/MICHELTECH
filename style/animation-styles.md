# Animation Styles

---

## 2D Hand-Drawn (Traditional)
- **Look:** Pencil/ink lines, painted backgrounds, fluid character animation, visible human touch
- **Sub-styles:**
  - **Disney Classic:** Clean lines, lush backgrounds, expressive character animation (Bambi, Lion King)
  - **Studio Ghibli:** Painterly backgrounds, naturalistic movement, detailed environments, quiet moments (Spirited Away, Totoro)
  - **Independent/Experimental:** Rougher lines, unique voices, less polished (The Triplets of Belleville, Persepolis)
- **Mood:** Warmth, artistry, timelessness, handmade beauty
- **Prompt language:** "hand-drawn animation, 2D animated, painted backgrounds, traditional animation, [sub-style]"

## 3D CG Animation
- **Look:** Computer-generated, range from stylized to photorealistic
- **Sub-styles:**
  - **Pixar/Disney 3D:** Stylized realism, expressive faces, rich lighting, detailed textures
  - **DreamWorks:** Slightly more exaggerated, comedic, broader expressions
  - **Hyperrealistic CG:** Uncanny valley territory, photorealistic rendering (Lion King 2019)
  - **Stylized 3D:** Non-photorealistic rendering, painterly, cel-shaded, Spider-Verse territory
- **Mood:** Modern, accessible, expressive, ranges from cartoony to photorealistic
- **Prompt language:** "3D animated, Pixar-style, CG rendering, [stylized/photorealistic], ray-traced lighting"

## Stop-Motion
- **Look:** Physical objects/puppets animated frame by frame. Visible texture, slight jitter, tangible materials
- **Sub-styles:**
  - **Claymation:** Clay figures (Wallace & Gromit, Early Man)
  - **Puppet:** Articulated puppets (Coraline, Fantastic Mr. Fox, Kubo)
  - **Object:** Found objects animated (PES, Jan Švankmajer)
  - **Paper/Cutout:** Paper figures and backgrounds (South Park original, many indie)
- **Mood:** Tactile, charming, handcrafted, slightly eerie (uncanny physicality)
- **Prompt language:** "stop-motion animation, claymation, puppet animation, tactile, handcrafted, visible texture, miniature set"

## Anime
- **Look:** Large expressive eyes, dynamic action lines, speed lines, color-coded hair, detailed backgrounds vs simpler characters
- **Sub-styles:**
  - **Shonen:** Action-focused, dynamic poses, energy effects (Naruto, Dragon Ball, Demon Slayer)
  - **Seinen:** Mature, detailed, darker (Berserk, Ghost in the Shell, Akira)
  - **Shojo:** Romantic, soft, sparkly, emotional (Sailor Moon, Fruits Basket)
  - **Makoto Shinkai:** Photorealistic backgrounds, lens flare, weather/sky porn, emotional depth (Your Name, Weathering With You)
  - **Satoshi Kon:** Surreal, psychological, reality-bending (Paprika, Perfect Blue)
  - **Masaaki Yuasa:** Fluid, shape-shifting, experimental movement (Mind Game, Devilman Crybaby)
- **Mood:** Depends on sub-style — ranges from action-epic to intimate-emotional to surreal
- **Prompt language:** "anime style, [sub-style], Japanese animation, [character/environment focus], cel-shaded"

## Rotoscope
- **Look:** Live action traced/painted over. Hyperreal movement with illustrated surface. Uncanny, dreamlike.
- **Mood:** Blurred reality, psychedelic, philosophical, literally between live and animated
- **Use when:** Drug sequences, reality-questioning stories, blending real and imagined
- **Prompt language:** "rotoscope animation, painted over live action, A Scanner Darkly style, wavering edges, surreal realism"
- **Reference:** A Scanner Darkly, Waking Life, Undone

## Motion Graphics / Kinetic
- **Look:** Typography, shapes, icons, infographics in motion. Abstract or semi-abstract.
- **Mood:** Informational, energetic, modern, brand-oriented
- **Use when:** Explainers, data visualization, title sequences, brand content, abstract concepts
- **Prompt language:** "motion graphics, kinetic typography, abstract shapes, clean design, animated infographic"

## Mixed Media
- **Look:** Combining live action with animation, collage, different media in one frame
- **Mood:** Experimental, playful, meta, genre-defying
- **Use when:** Music videos, experimental shorts, breaking reality, commentary
- **Prompt language:** "mixed media, live action combined with animation, collage style, multimedia, experimental"
- **Reference:** Spider-Verse, Gorillaz videos, Terry Gilliam animations

## Pixel Art / 8-bit / 16-bit
- **Look:** Grid-based, limited color palette, chunky pixels, game-nostalgic
- **Mood:** Retro gaming, nostalgia, indie, chiptune, charming limitation
- **Use when:** Gaming content, retro nostalgia, indie aesthetic, playful
- **Prompt language:** "pixel art, 8-bit, 16-bit, retro game style, limited color palette, pixelated"

## Watercolor / Painterly Animation
- **Look:** Soft edges, visible brushstrokes, color bleeds, paint texture
- **Mood:** Gentle, artistic, dreamlike, storybook, emotional
- **Use when:** Children's content, art pieces, gentle storytelling, music videos
- **Prompt language:** "watercolor animation, painted, soft brushstrokes, color bleeding, storybook illustration, gentle"

## Lo-Fi / Indie Web Animation
- **Look:** Intentionally rough, simple shapes, limited animation, internet culture
- **Mood:** DIY, absurdist, underground, meme-adjacent, anti-polish
- **Use when:** Comedy, internet culture, anti-establishment, absurdist humor
- **Prompt language:** "lo-fi animation, crude drawing, internet animation, simple shapes, rough, indie"
- **Reference:** Adult Swim bumps, Don Hertzfeldt, early Newgrounds
