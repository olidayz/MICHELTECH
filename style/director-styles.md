# Director Visual Signatures

Recognizable visual languages of key directors. Useful for referencing and prompting.

---

## Wes Anderson
- **Signature:** Dead-center symmetry, planimetric framing, pastel palette, miniature/dollhouse feeling, whip pans, overhead inserts
- **Prompt language:** "Wes Anderson style, centered symmetry, pastel colors, planimetric composition, dollhouse, whimsical"

## Stanley Kubrick
- **Signature:** One-point perspective (hallway shots), cold symmetry, wide-angle lens, slow zoom, unsettling precision
- **Prompt language:** "Kubrick style, one-point perspective, symmetrical hallway, cold, precise, wide-angle, unsettling calm"

## Wong Kar-wai
- **Signature:** Step-printing (smeared motion), neon reflections, rain, saturated color, frames within frames, melancholic beauty
- **Prompt language:** "Wong Kar-wai style, neon reflections, smeared motion blur, saturated color, rain, In the Mood for Love, melancholic"

## Terrence Malick
- **Signature:** Golden hour obsession, wide-angle close-ups, nature intercuts, whispered VO, handheld intimacy, magic hour light
- **Prompt language:** "Terrence Malick style, golden hour, wide-angle close-up, nature, whispered, ethereal, magic hour light"

## David Lynch
- **Signature:** Surreal mundanity, velvet darkness, red curtains, industrial sound, uncanny everyday, dream logic
- **Prompt language:** "David Lynch style, surreal, uncanny, velvet darkness, red curtains, suburban nightmare, dream logic, unsettling"

## Denis Villeneuve
- **Signature:** Massive scale with intimate emotion, muted palette, haze/fog, geometric architecture, overwhelming environments
- **Prompt language:** "Denis Villeneuve style, massive scale, muted palette, haze, geometric, monumental, Blade Runner 2049, Dune"

## Spike Lee
- **Signature:** Dutch angles, double dolly (character floating), bold color, confrontational framing, direct-to-camera address
- **Prompt language:** "Spike Lee style, dutch angle, bold color, confrontational, double dolly, direct to camera, Do the Right Thing"

## Akira Kurosawa
- **Signature:** Weather as emotion (rain, wind, fog), deep staging, wipe transitions, movement in depth, patient framing
- **Prompt language:** "Kurosawa style, rain and wind, deep staging, dramatic weather, patient composition, samurai, Seven Samurai"

## Ridley Scott
- **Signature:** Smoke/atmosphere in every frame, practical lighting, rain, industrial texture, scale, lived-in worlds
- **Prompt language:** "Ridley Scott style, atmospheric smoke, rain, industrial, practical lighting, lived-in, dense atmosphere, Alien, Blade Runner"

## Greta Gerwig
- **Signature:** Warm natural light, soft palette, movement-driven shots, golden-tone nostalgia, Sacramento pink-orange
- **Prompt language:** "Greta Gerwig style, warm natural light, soft palette, golden nostalgia, Lady Bird, Sacramento tones, intimate"

## Christopher Nolan
- **Signature:** IMAX scale, practical effects, cross-cutting timelines, desaturated realism, overwhelming sound
- **Prompt language:** "Christopher Nolan style, IMAX, practical, massive scale, desaturated, cross-cutting, Inception, Interstellar"

## Hayao Miyazaki
- **Signature:** Hand-drawn lush environments, flying sequences, food porn, quiet contemplation moments, nature worship, no pure villains
- **Prompt language:** "Miyazaki style, Studio Ghibli, hand-painted backgrounds, lush nature, flying, gentle, magical realism"

## Quentin Tarantino
- **Signature:** Trunk shot, low angle, long dialogue scenes, sudden violence, retro soundtrack, chapter titles, exploitation homage
- **Prompt language:** "Tarantino style, trunk shot, low angle, retro, exploitation cinema, bold dialogue, stylized violence"

## Sofia Coppola
- **Signature:** Pastel palette, isolation in luxury, dreamy, female gaze, pop music against period, soft focus, ennui
- **Prompt language:** "Sofia Coppola style, pastel, dreamy, isolation, soft focus, female gaze, Lost in Translation, Marie Antoinette"

## Roger Deakins (Cinematographer)
- **Signature:** Natural light obsession, motivated lighting, single-source, clean composition, every frame a painting
- **Prompt language:** "Roger Deakins cinematography, natural light, motivated single source, clean composition, painterly, Blade Runner 2049, 1917"
