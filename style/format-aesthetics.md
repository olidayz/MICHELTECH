# Format & Camera Aesthetics

Looks defined by the recording medium, not the content. Each format carries built-in associations.

---

## VHS / Betamax
- **Look:** Tracking lines, warm color bleed, soft focus, interlaced scan lines, tape distortion, low resolution
- **Mood:** Nostalgia, 80s/90s childhood, horror (found footage), warmth, memory
- **Use when:** Nostalgia content, horror, retro branding, "remember when" feeling, music videos
- **Prompt language:** "VHS aesthetic, tracking lines, tape distortion, warm color bleed, scan lines, low resolution, 1980s home video"

## Old iPhone / Early Smartphone
- **Look:** Low-fi, small sensor noise, slightly washed out, auto-exposure shifting, intimate framing
- **Mood:** Authentic, personal, intimate, "real," user-generated feel
- **Use when:** Authenticity matters, social proof, first-person POV, making something feel unproduced
- **Prompt language:** "shot on iPhone, low-fi, slightly overexposed, natural light, handheld, personal, candid"

## 8mm / Super 8
- **Look:** Heavy grain, warm tones, occasional light leaks, slightly overexposed, dreamlike motion blur
- **Mood:** Memory, home movies, childhood, innocence, the past through rose-tinted lens
- **Use when:** Flashbacks, memory sequences, nostalgic montage, indie warmth
- **Prompt language:** "Super 8 film, heavy grain, warm tones, light leak, overexposed, home movie feel, 1970s"

## 16mm Film
- **Look:** Visible grain, warm or cool depending on stock, slightly soft, organic texture, indie
- **Mood:** Independent, artistic, documentary, authentic, textured
- **Use when:** Indie feel, documentary, art film, textured realism, period pieces
- **Prompt language:** "16mm film, visible grain, organic texture, slightly soft focus, indie cinematography"

## 35mm Film
- **Look:** Fine grain, rich color, deep blacks, classic cinema feel, natural skin tones
- **Mood:** Cinema. The standard by which everything else is measured.
- **Use when:** Anything that should feel like a "real movie," classic storytelling
- **Prompt language:** "35mm film, rich color, cinematic, fine grain, deep blacks, film photography"

## IMAX / Large Format
- **Look:** Extreme clarity, resolution, detail, epic scale, immersive
- **Mood:** Awe, spectacle, importance, you-are-there immersion
- **Use when:** Epic landscapes, action, space, anything meant to overwhelm
- **Prompt language:** "IMAX quality, ultra-sharp, extreme detail, epic scale, immersive, large format"

## GoPro / Action Cam
- **Look:** Wide/fisheye, first-person, barrel distortion, high frame rate, action-oriented
- **Mood:** Energy, adrenaline, immersion, extreme sports, adventure
- **Use when:** POV action, sports, adventure, first-person experience
- **Prompt language:** "GoPro POV, wide-angle fisheye, action camera, first-person perspective, high energy"

## Drone / Aerial
- **Look:** Bird's eye, sweeping landscapes, slow reveals, geographic context, scale
- **Mood:** Freedom, scale, god's view, establishing, beauty
- **Use when:** Establishing shots, travel content, scale reveals, nature
- **Prompt language:** "aerial drone shot, bird's eye view, sweeping landscape, high altitude, cinematic"

## Polaroid / Instant Film
- **Look:** Faded colors, white border, soft focus, square format, unique color rendition
- **Mood:** Nostalgia, snapshot, precious moment captured, impermanent
- **Use when:** Memory, nostalgia, collected moments, album-like sequences
- **Prompt language:** "Polaroid photo, faded colors, white border, instant film, vintage, soft"

## Disposable Camera
- **Look:** Flash, grain, slightly off-color, red-eye, party vibes, unpredictable
- **Mood:** Youth, party, carefree, raw, accidental beauty
- **Use when:** Youth culture, parties, "night out" content, raw social moments
- **Prompt language:** "disposable camera flash, grainy, slightly overexposed, party, nighttime, amateur photography"

## Surveillance / CCTV
- **Look:** Fixed wide angle, high position, grainy, timestamp overlay, slightly green or B&W
- **Mood:** Voyeuristic, tension, observed, paranoia, found footage
- **Use when:** Thriller, horror, privacy themes, Big Brother, something-watching-you feeling
- **Prompt language:** "CCTV surveillance camera, fixed wide angle, grainy, timestamp overlay, high angle, security footage"

## Webcam / Video Call
- **Look:** Front-facing, slightly above eye level, screen-lit face, compressed, flat
- **Mood:** Isolation, connection-through-screens, pandemic era, confession, intimacy through distance
- **Use when:** Modern isolation stories, video diary, screen-life films, talking head
- **Prompt language:** "webcam footage, front-facing camera, screen-lit face, slightly above eye level, video call quality"

## Thermal Camera
- **Look:** Heat signature colors (blues/blacks cold, reds/yellows warm), body outlines, alien perspective
- **Mood:** Predatory, surveillance, alien perception, scientific
- **Use when:** Sci-fi, horror (Predator), military, seeing the unseen
- **Prompt language:** "thermal imaging camera, heat signature, infrared, blues and reds, body heat glow"
