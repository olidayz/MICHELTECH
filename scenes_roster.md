## USAGE

When generating content:

1. **Pick a mode** (Mashup, Direct, Newsjack, Meme, Remix)
2. **Select scene** from roster that fits the political topic
3. **Plug in character** from character bible
4. **Adapt the dialogue** to the political context
5. **Add the twist** - the comedic payoff

Example:
- Mode: REMIX
- Scene: The Lion King - Circle of Life  
- Character: Macron
- Topic: Retirement reform
- Result: Macron on cliff, animals = French citizens, sunset = his legacy setting
